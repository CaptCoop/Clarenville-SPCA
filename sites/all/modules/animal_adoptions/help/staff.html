Staff

This section deals with all the Staff functions you have access to when logged in as a staff member.

Staff members can administer most everything that is on the site. They have a wide variety of functions available to them.

Permissions

Staff members have permission to:

  • add, edit or delete anything that they have created
  • edit and administer most anything on the site no matter who created it
  • access any user account

For security reasons, Staff accounts will be automatically logged out after 4 hours of inactivity.

For any action that a staff member cannot perform, see your Webmaster.
