Edit an existing Veterinary Clinic

To edit existing vet details, simply follow these steps:

  • Click on the "Content" menu item in your management menu, to bring up all of the created content on the website
  • Select the Veterinarian Office entry you want to edit.
  • Click the "Edit" tab located above the Veterinarian details.
  • You will be presented with the same screen you see when adding a new Veterinarian Office.
  • Change or add to the information on file, then click the save button at the bottom of the screen.
