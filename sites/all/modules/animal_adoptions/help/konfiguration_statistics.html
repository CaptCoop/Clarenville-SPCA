Statistics

Settings for the statistical information that Drupal will keep about the site. This tracks such data as

  • Who is visiting your site
  • What files, animal profiles and other pages they have visited
  • From when and where they are visiting

Access log settings

Discard access logs older than. A dropdown with the length of time Drupal should keep tracking records for. This tracking feature is only meant to give you a snapshot in time of what's trending on the website. Long term data gets tracked in much more detail in Google Analytics. Therefore, it is recommended not to set this feature any longer than 1-4 weeks in duration.
