Delete a Term in a Existing Vocabulary

To delete a term in a vocabulary, do the following:

  • From the Management Navigation Bar, go to Structure > Taxonomy
  • Click the list terms link on the vocabulary name you want to edit the terms of. This will display all the current terms under that vocabulary and give you the option to edit each one
  • Click the edit link for the term you want to delete
  • Click the red DELETE button
  • You will be presented with a deletion conformation page. Click the delete button again to confirm.
