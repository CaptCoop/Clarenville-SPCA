Content

Content is anything you can read, watch or see on your website. It is what makes up the majority of everything that IS, your website.

Manage Content Page

By clicking on the Content link in your Management Navigation Bar, you will be displayed with a dashboard style, administration page. Here, you can add, edit or in some cases, delete content from your site if you have the proper permissions to do so.

You can interact with any Content items that have green links.
