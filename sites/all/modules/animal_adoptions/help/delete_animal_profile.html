Delete an Existing Animal Profile

NOTE: This website is designed to track activity, mine data and keep long, intricate histories of the animals you deal with on a daily basis. Deleting vital information from the website may cause unintended results. Please delete with care.

To delete an existing animal profile, simply follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the animal profile you want to delete.
  • Click the "Edit" tab located above the animal picture
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the red DELETE button. You will be presented with a delete confirmation page.
  • To complete the delete, press the red DELETE button again
