Administer an Adoption Request

Any new applications that arrive into the system will be automatically marked with a application status of "Waiting". All waiting applications are new applications that have not been administered yet.

Once you access an application, you should change the status to "In Review" and save. This will let you know that this application has been accessed and is being looked at by someone.

An application should stay in review until a decision has been made on whether or not the applicant qualifies to adopt the animal. During this review, the notes should be kept up to date in order to capture the entire history of the application.

Once a decision has been made on the file, you should change the assign status to reflect that decision (Approved/Rejected), make any required notes, and save.

If the application was approved, then once the user has picked up their adopted animal. Capture this activity in the notes section, then change the assigned status to Closed.
