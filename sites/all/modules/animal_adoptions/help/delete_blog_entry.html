Delete an existing Blog Entry

NOTE: This website is designed to track activity, mine data and keep long, intricate histories of user activity on a daily basis. Deleting vital information from the website may cause unintended results. Please delete with care.

To delete an existing blog entry, simply follow these steps:

  • Navigate to the blog entry you want to delete.
  • Click the "Edit" tab located above the blog picture, near the top of the screen.
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and click the red DELETE button. You will be presented with a delete confirmation page.
  • To complete the delete, press the red DELETE button again
